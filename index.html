Our Donate Plan

Our main goal is to provide food, shelter and medicines to people in need. We will also build temporary education program for affected children

Food for a family

There is huge shortage of food in the affected area. We will provide a food package consist of basic foods to every family in the floody area.

Shelter for a family

Flood destroy almost every house in the affected area. People do not have enough shelter to accomodate themselves and their animals. Our goal is to provide temporary shelter for each family.

Educatiion for a child

Most of the educational infrastructures are destroyed by heavy flood and remainings are unable of to use due to heavy rainfall. We will open education camp in the area and provide basic education to the young children.
